Refund & Cancellation Policy

When refunds and cancellations are available for products, workshops, coaching programs and memberships.

Last updated: May 12, 2026

We want you to get real value from what you buy. This policy explains when refunds and cancellations are available and how to request one.

1. Free ALIGN session

The ALIGN session is offered at no cost. There is no charge and therefore no refund. You may cancel or reschedule at any time before the call by replying to your booking email.

2. Digital products (books, workbooks, mini-courses, recorded workshops)

Digital products are delivered immediately upon purchase. Because content is consumed quickly and cannot be returned, all sales are final, subject to the exceptions below:

  • If the file or course was not delivered for a technical reason on our side, we will redeliver it or refund the full purchase price.
  • If you bought the wrong product by mistake and have not downloaded, opened or accessed it, contact us within 7 days for a refund.

3. Live workshops and challenges (single sessions)

  • Full refund if you cancel at least 7 days before the start date.
  • 50% refund if you cancel between 6 days and 24 hours before the start date.
  • No refund after the workshop or challenge has started; the recording (where applicable) will be made available to you.
  • If we cancel a session, you may transfer to another date at no cost or receive a full refund.

4. Coaching programs (Build, Scale, CEO Freedom Partnership, CEO Collective and similar)

Coaching programs require time and capacity to be reserved for you and are governed by a separate written engagement letter or program agreement. In the absence of a different rule set out in that agreement:

  • You may cancel within 14 days of payment for a full refund, provided no more than one session has taken place and no advanced materials have been downloaded.
  • After 14 days, refunds are not available. You may end the engagement at any time, but remaining instalments under a payment plan continue to be due in accordance with your agreement.
  • Missed sessions are not refundable but can be rescheduled with at least 24 hours’ notice, subject to availability.
  • If we cancel the program for reasons other than your breach, we will refund the unused portion on a pro rata basis.

5. Memberships and recurring subscriptions

  • You can cancel a recurring subscription at any time from your account, or by emailing us at least 5 business days before the next billing date.
  • Cancellation stops future charges. Past payments are non-refundable, but you keep access until the end of the period you paid for.

6. How to request a refund

  1. Email forand.dominique@gmail.com from the address you used to purchase, within the applicable window above.
  2. Include your name, the product or program purchased, the date of purchase and the reason.
  3. We will reply within 5 business days and, if approved, process the refund to your original payment method within 10 business days. Your bank may take additional time to display the credit.

7. Chargebacks

If something is wrong with your order, please reach out to us first — we will almost always resolve it faster than a chargeback. Filing a chargeback for a charge you authorized can be considered an act of fraud and may result in collection action and termination of any active engagement.

8. Quebec consumers

Nothing in this policy limits the rights granted to consumers by the Quebec Consumer Protection Act or any other applicable law. Quebec consumers may always contact the Office de la protection du consommateur (opc.gouv.qc.ca) for information about their rights.
